# Research brief: Interviews

## Background
Today, people continue to use manual or passive methods to find new podcast shows.

## Research goals
1. Better understand current behavior (triangulate with secondary research findings)
2. Test early concepts that include different formats (i.e. a physical device)

## Research questions
1. What our their current podcast listening behaviors?
   2. Triggers (what reminds them to listen)
   3. Context (how, where, when, how often)
   4. Content (show types, lengths, appeal)
2. How do they discover new shows?
3. How do they decide to continue listening to a new show?
4. How might they see themselves using the concepts presented to them?

## Methodology
Virtual 1hr long semi-structured interviews with 4-6 North American based participants with a mix of demographics.

## Recruitment criteria
Aim to have a mix of gender, age, and ethnicities
1. (Established behavior) Listens to 1-2 podcasts/week
2. (Disposable income) TK
